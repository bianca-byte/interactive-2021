a dialogue between
you and your things







Read the steps below, once you’ve gathered your thoughts on each step, pre-record your findings with an audio recorder. What you say will take the place of images, your words are the images. The audio recordings will be gathered and made available on this website. When submitting your recording, provide an alias name that you would like your recording to be stored under.



  1. you
    using language (doesn’t have to be one), describe an object that belongs to you. Why did you choose this?

  2. your home
    now describe something about your home, could be an object that means something, could be anything.

  3. the street
    now move onto the street, find a thing that draws your attention, now describe it. can be a view from your window, or you can take a walk and explore.

  4. the neighborhood
    moving beyond the street, describe a thing that draws your attention, something situated within your neighborhood, can be something new you stumble across, or something from memory.
